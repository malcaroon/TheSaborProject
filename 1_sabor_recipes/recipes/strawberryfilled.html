Strawberry-Filled Donut Recipe

Soft and pillowy donuts filled with sweet strawberry jam, then dusted with powdered sugar. A fruity, delightful option that adds a burst of flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

Donut Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons yeast
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup butter, softened
  • Oil for frying

Filling & Topping

  • 1 cup strawberry jam
  • ½ cup confectioners sugar

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, yeast, and salt.
  2. Add warm milk, eggs, and softened butter. Knead until smooth and elastic, about 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Cover and let dough rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until doubled in size.
  4. Roll dough to ½-inch thick and cut into rounds. Place on a baking sheet, cover, and let rise again for 30 minutes.
  5. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C). Fry donuts until golden brown, turning once, about 2 to 3 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  6. Fill a piping bag with strawberry jam. Insert tip into the side of each donut and squeeze gently to fill.
  7. Dust donuts with confectioners sugar before serving.
