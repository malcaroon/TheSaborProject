Classic Glazed Donut Recipe

Light, fluffy, and golden brown, these donuts are fried to perfection and dipped in a sweet vanilla glaze. A timeless treat that is simple yet irresistible.

Ingredients

Donut Dough:

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • Oil for frying

Glaze

  • 1 cup confectioners sugar
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk milk, melted butter, vanilla, and egg until smooth.
  3. Add wet mixture to dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Dough should be soft but not sticky.
  4. Roll dough on a floured surface to about ½-inch thick. Cut with a donut cutter (or use a round cutter plus a small cap for the hole).
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan to 350°F (175°C).
  6. Fry donuts in batches, 1 to 2 minutes per side, until golden brown. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  7. For glaze: Mix confectioners sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth. Dip warm donuts into glaze and let set before serving.
