Chocolate Frosted Donut Recipe

Rich and indulgent, these donuts combine cocoa in the batter with a smooth chocolate frosting on top. Perfect for chocolate lovers who want a decadent twist on the classic.

Ingredients

Donut Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Oil for frying

Chocolate Frosting

  • 1 cup confectioners sugar
  • ¼ cup cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl.
  2. In another bowl, whisk buttermilk, eggs, melted butter, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. Add wet mixture to dry ingredients and stir until combined.
  4. Roll dough on a floured surface and cut into donut shapes.
  5. Heat oil to 350°F (175°C) and fry donuts until cooked through, about 2 minutes per side. Drain on paper towels.
  6. For frosting: Mix confectioners sugar, cocoa powder, milk, and vanilla until smooth.
  7. Dip cooled donuts into frosting and let set before serving.
